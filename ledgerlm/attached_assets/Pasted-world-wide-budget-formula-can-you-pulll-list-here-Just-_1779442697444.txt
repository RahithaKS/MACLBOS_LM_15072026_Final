Here are the standard worldwide financial budget formulas used across global organisations:

Core Budget Formulas
#	Formula Name	Formula	Used For
1	Budget Variance	Actual − Budget	How much over/under budget
2	Budget Variance %	(Actual − Budget) ÷ Budget × 100	% deviation from plan
3	Budget Utilisation %	Actual ÷ Budget × 100	How much of budget is consumed
4	Remaining Budget	Budget − Actual	What's left to spend
5	Forecast to Complete	Remaining Budget − Committed Costs	Expected spend on remaining work
6	Full Year Forecast (FYF)	YTD Actual + Remaining Budget	Projected end-of-year spend
7	Run Rate	YTD Actual ÷ Elapsed Months	Average monthly spend
8	Annualised Run Rate	Run Rate × 12	Full year projection at current pace
9	Budget Absorption Rate	YTD Actual ÷ YTD Budget × 100	Pace of spending vs plan
10	Cost to Budget Ratio	Actual ÷ Budget	Ratio form of utilisation
Month-on-Month Formulas
#	Formula Name	Formula	Used For
11	MoM Actual	YTD(current month) − YTD(previous month)	True monthly cost from YTD data
12	MoM Growth %	(MoM current − MoM previous) ÷ MoM previous × 100	Monthly trend
13	MoM Budget	Budget YTD(current) − Budget YTD(previous)	Monthly budget allocation
14	MoM Variance	MoM Actual − MoM Budget	Monthly over/under
Year-on-Year Formulas
#	Formula Name	Formula	Used For
15	YoY Growth %	(Current Year − Prior Year) ÷ Prior Year × 100	Annual comparison
16	YoY Variance	Current Year Actual − Prior Year Actual	Absolute change
17	YoY Budget Change %	(Current Budget − Prior Budget) ÷ Prior Budget × 100	Budget growth rate
Capacity & Utilisation Formulas
#	Formula Name	Formula	Used For
18	Internal Utilisation %	Billed Capacity ÷ Allocated Capacity × 100	MS/SX utilisation (Bosch)
19	Capacity Variance	Allocated Capacity − Billed Capacity	Unused capacity
20	Average Capacity	Total Capacity ÷ Number of Months	Average over a period
21	Headcount Cost	Total OS Cost ÷ Headcount	Cost per person
22	Offshore/Onsite Ratio	Offshore HC ÷ Total HC × 100	Delivery mix
Cost Efficiency Formulas
#	Formula Name	Formula	Used For
23	Cost per Unit	Total Cost ÷ Units Produced/Delivered	Efficiency measure
24	Savings vs Budget	Budget − Actual (positive = saving)	Cost savings tracking
25	CAGR	(End Value ÷ Start Value)^(1÷Years) − 1	Compound annual growth
26	Break-even Point	Fixed Costs ÷ (Revenue − Variable Cost per Unit)	Profitability threshold
27	ROI	(Net Benefit − Cost) ÷ Cost × 100	Return on investment
28	Cost Variance Index (CVI)	Earned Value ÷ Actual Cost	Project cost efficiency

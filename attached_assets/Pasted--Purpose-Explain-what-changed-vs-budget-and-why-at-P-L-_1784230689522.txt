**Purpose**

Explain *what changed* vs budget and *why* — at P&L, cost center, product, and account level.

**Primary users**

FP&A, CFO, Business Finance, BU Heads

---

**Data inputs**

- `Actuals` (GL / trial balance, by month, cost center, account)
- `Budget` (same granularity as actuals)
- Optional: `Forecast`, `Volume / Qty`, `Price`, `FX rate`, `Headcount`

**Core metrics / logic**

- Absolute variance = `Actual – Budget`
- % variance = `(Actual – Budget) / Budget`
- Categorization by:
    - Revenue: **Volume / Price / Mix** variance
    - COGS: purchase price, yield, efficiency
    - Opex: **Headcount vs non-headcount**, fixed vs variable
- Variance tagging: `favorable / unfavorable / neutral`
- Thresholds: e.g., “show items > ±5% or > ₹X”

---

**Standard views**

1. **P&L Variance Bridge (Company level)**
2. **Cost Center Variance View**
3. **Product / BU Variance View**
4. **Drill-down Explorer** → GL → transaction-level

---

**LedgerLM AI features**

- **Assist mode**
    - Auto-generate variance commentary:
        - “Revenue is 7% below budget mainly due to lower volumes in Region West (-12%) despite higher price realization (+3%).”
    - Suggest where to investigate:
        - “Top 5 variance drivers by impact.”
- **Auto mode**
    - Tag root causes (e.g., demand drop vs price vs operational issues)
    - Auto-prepare “Month-end BvA Pack” as PowerPoint / PDF.
- **Example prompts**
    - “Explain why EBITDA missed budget in September.”
